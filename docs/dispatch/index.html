Yesterday, I had tapas and wine with a few friends. It was a really lovely time. They are particularly inspiring because I think they are some wicked smart folks that love their craft. Why do we spend so much of our time making money for companies?

My goal for so long was to build stuff. Stuff that was useful and valuable to the world.

My friend, and former co-worker, told me how the CEO always starts the weekly meeting with how the team is trying to add value for landlords and to make the industry better. But, at the end of the day, my friend felt like all they do is try to make their users into customers. Users becoming customers is a good thing, but they shouldn't be duped into it.

It's super fucked. But I think you need to hear this for yourself.

This is yet another thing.

This is another thing.
